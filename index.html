FAQ

Answers to common questions from creators, teams, and clients.

Does it record across apps?

Yes — Screen Flow Pro uses iOS screen broadcast so recordings can capture activity across apps. Enable Do Not Disturb to avoid unexpected notifications.

What are Sessions?

Each recording is saved as a Session in your Library so you can review, add markers, apply titles, and keep metadata organized.

How do timeline markers work?

While reviewing, move the playhead and add a marker at the selected time. Markers become timestamped highlights for faster reviews.

What is Bug Report mode (Pro)?

A Pro workflow that produces a shareable bug report with a timecoded transcript (.VTT) plus a marker summary for key moments.

Which export formats are included?

Export profiles include raw video, MOV (with settings in Pro), and social presets like TikTok (9:16), Instagram Reels (9:16), Square (1:1), and 4:5 feed formats.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. App Store subscriptions are tied to your Apple ID.
