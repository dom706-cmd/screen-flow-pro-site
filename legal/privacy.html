What Screen Flow Pro does

Screen Flow Pro records your screen using iOS screen broadcast and organizes recordings into Sessions so you can review, add markers, and export in different formats.

Data handling (template)

  • Recordings: Saved on-device unless you choose to share/export them.
  • Microphone audio: Optional during recording.
  • Exports: When you share an export, the destination (Messages, Mail, Drive, etc.) is controlled by that app/service.
  • Purchases: Subscriptions are handled by the App Store. The app does not receive your payment details.
